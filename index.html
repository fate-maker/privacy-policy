Privacy Policy - Dreal

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

At Dreal, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Pinterest automation application.

2. Information We Collect

Pinterest Data:

  • Basic account information (username, profile details)
  • Your pins, boards, and content
  • Performance analytics and engagement metrics
  • Publishing permissions to create and manage pins

Technical Data:

  • API access tokens (encrypted and secure)
  • Usage logs for troubleshooting
  • App performance metrics

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Automated Publishing: Schedule and publish pins on your behalf
  • Content Analysis: Analyze pin performance and optimize strategy
  • Competitor Insights: Provide analytics on competitor performance (anonymous data only)
  • Service Improvement: Enhance app features and user experience

4. Data Protection & Security

  • We implement industry-standard encryption for all data
  • Your Pinterest credentials are never stored on our servers
  • We use secure OAuth 2.0 authentication with Pinterest
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Data minimization - we only collect what's necessary

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We only share data with:

  • Pinterest: As required for API functionality
  • Service Providers: Secure cloud infrastructure (encrypted data only)

6. Your Rights

  • Access and download your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke app access at any time via Pinterest settings
  • Opt-out of data collection (may limit app functionality)

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request immediate deletion by contacting us.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices. Continued use of our app constitutes acceptance of updated terms.
